proper.tea | Javascript application designed to provide my userbase the proper type of tea for their current or desired mood. Users are able to input their current mood and 'proper.tea' will return a selection of various teas including brands, reviews, pricing, sources for purchase, and their natural properties. Users are able to create an account, save, and update their favorite tea selections, and post reviews to help other users to find their preferred 'cup of tea'.
